**Sales Analysis Dashboard**



**Overview**



This project analyzes retail sales data to identify trends, evaluate product performance, and understand regional sales distribution. The goal is to transform raw sales transactions into meaningful insights using Python-based data analysis techniques.

The project includes exploratory data analysis, data visualization, and a simple sales forecast to demonstrate core data analyst skills.





**Tools \& Technologies**

* Pandas
* NumPy
* Matplotlib
* Seaborn
* Scikit-learn
* Jupyter Notebook

**Dataset**



The dataset represents simulated retail sales transactions and includes:

* Date – transaction date
* Product – product sold
* Region – sales region
* Quantity – number of items sold
* Unit Price – price per item
* Sales – total transaction value



**Analysis Performed**



**Sales Trend Analysis**



Sales were aggregated by month to identify trends and visualize how revenue changes over time.



**Product Performance**



Products were analyzed to determine the top contributors to overall revenue.



**Regional Sales Distribution**



Sales were grouped by region to understand which geographic areas drive the most revenue.



**Sales Forecast**



A simple regression model was used to estimate future sales based on historical trends.



**Key Insights**



* Monthly sales analysis highlights fluctuations in revenue and potential seasonal patterns.
* A small number of products contribute a large share of total revenue, indicating key revenue drivers.
* Certain regions generate significantly higher sales, suggesting stronger market demand in those areas.
* Basic predictive modeling shows how historical trends can be used to estimate short-term future sales.
